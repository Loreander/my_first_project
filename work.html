Math Teacher - August 2010-June 2018

I have been working at Milton High School for the past eight years. As a math educator I have taught Algebra, Geometry, Statistics, Algebra II, and AP Calculus. My experiences also include being a team leader, graduation speaker, and a coach. As a team leader I organized and facilitated cross-curricular meetings that focused on student needs and improving the learning community. I have coached the Golf Team (2017), Math Team (2011-2018), and Ultimate Frisbee Team (2017-2018). I have been nominated for teacher of the year for the past two years. As an educator I had to work in a team setting, meet the needs of my 'clients', and work in a constantly changing landscape. Check out my graduation speech to the right!


Colchester School Board - August 2009- June 2010

As a senior member of the student planning team at Colchester High School, I was also selected to be a student member of the Colchester School Board. Although I was a non-voting member of the school board I learned all about the intricate relationships that exist between community, school board, and teachers/staff. This experience still impacts me to this day when dealing with difficult situations and groups of people with different beliefs.

Restaurant Employee / Manager - March 2004-January 2010

New York Pizza Oven in Colchester was my first job as a high school student. After a few years I became a manager of the restaurant and would open/close while advising the owner on scheduling and hiring decisions. Having this level of responsibility at a young age was crucial to improving my work ethic and realizing how small businesses function.
